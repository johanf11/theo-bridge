# Theo Bridge — Loom Demo Script

**Target length:** 2:30–3:00
**Format:** Screen recording with voiceover (face cam optional)
**Audience:** SCF reviewers, investors, Stellar ecosystem

---

## Pre-recording Setup

- Log in to the Theo demo account (NABATCO / admin user)
- Make sure the Dashboard has recent transactions and non-zero balances
- Have the Convert page ready with a fresh quote (don't submit yet)
- Browser at 100% zoom, clean tab bar, no bookmarks bar
- Close notifications and other apps

---

## Scene 1 — The Problem (0:00–0:20)

**Screen:** Start on a blank screen or the Theo landing page.

**Voiceover:**
> Haiti's importers need dollars to pay international suppliers. Today, they buy those dollars from informal money changers at a 5 to 15 percent markup — no receipts, no transparency, no recourse. Traditional banks ration dollar access, and there's no formal alternative for businesses moving ten million dollars a year or more. Theo changes that.

**Action:** Click "Get Started" or navigate to `/dashboard`.

---

## Scene 2 — Dashboard Overview (0:20–0:40)

**Screen:** Dashboard page — stat cards, volume chart, recent transactions visible.

**Voiceover:**
> This is Theo. A compliant dollar account for Haitian businesses, built on Stellar. The dashboard shows real-time USDC and HTG balances, monthly conversion volume, and a live transaction feed. Everything a treasury team needs in one view.

**Action:** Hover over the volume chart to show the period selector (7D / 30D / YTD). Point to the USDC balance card and the recent transactions table.

---

## Scene 3 — Core Flow: HTG to USDC (0:40–1:25)

**Screen:** Navigate to `/convert` — the HTG Deposit tab.

**Voiceover:**
> Here's how it works. A business deposits Haitian Gourdes and receives USDC in their Stellar wallet. Let me walk through it.
>
> I enter the amount in HTG — say, five hundred thousand gourdes. Theo pulls the live exchange rate directly from Haiti's central bank — the Banque de la Republique d'Haiti. The rate, the fees, and the net USDC amount are all shown transparently before the customer commits. No hidden spread.
>
> I submit the quote. The rate is locked for fifteen minutes. The customer gets SPIH wire instructions with a unique reference number.

**Action:**
1. Type `500000` in the HTG amount field
2. Point to the rate display and fee breakdown
3. Click "Get Quote"
4. Show the Order Status page — point to the reference number and SPIH instructions

**Voiceover (continued):**
> Once the HTG wire arrives, USDC is released to the customer's wallet in seconds — with a Stellar transaction hash on every order.

**Action:** Navigate to a completed order. Point to the Stellar tx hash and the "Download Receipt" button.

---

## Scene 4 — Payouts (1:25–1:45)

**Screen:** Navigate to `/payout`.

**Voiceover:**
> Once a business holds USDC, they can pay anyone instantly. Enter a recipient's Stellar address, the amount, and send. Settlement is three to five seconds, fees are fractions of a cent. This is how an importer pays a supplier in Miami or a freight company in the Dominican Republic — no wire delays, no correspondent bank fees.

**Action:** Show the payout form filled out. Point to the recipient field and amount. (Don't submit — or submit a small test amount.)

---

## Scene 5 — Supporting Features (1:45–2:10)

**Screen:** Quick navigation through multiple pages.

**Voiceover:**
> Theo isn't just a conversion tool — it's a full business banking layer.

**Action + Voiceover for each:**

1. **Navigate to `/invoices`:**
   > Businesses can create and send invoices in USDC or HTG, with QR code payment links.

2. **Navigate to `/compliance`:**
   > The compliance page shows live reserve proof — how much HTG-C is in circulation versus what's held in the treasury — all verifiable on the Stellar ledger.

3. **Navigate to `/billing`:**
   > Every fee is itemized and exportable as a PDF statement.

4. **Navigate to `/settings` (team tab):**
   > And teams can manage roles and permissions — Owner, Treasury Analyst, Viewer — so the right people have the right access.

---

## Scene 6 — Traction and Ask (2:10–2:40)

**Screen:** Return to the Dashboard, or show the landing page / a slide with key stats.

**Voiceover:**
> Theo has a signed MOU with NABATCO, a division of Groupe Acra — one of Haiti's largest industrial conglomerates. They convert over a hundred million dollars a year. They've committed to pilot with us on mainnet.
>
> Everything you just saw is live on Stellar Testnet today — twenty-one edge functions, twenty pages, the full order lifecycle running on real Stellar infrastructure. We built this in twelve days.
>
> The SCF Build Award funds the bridge from working prototype to mainnet launch — HTG-C deployed on mainnet, a live pilot with an enterprise customer doing real volume, and Theo listed as Haiti's first Stellar anchor.
>
> Haiti has no listed Stellar anchor today. We're here to change that.

**Action:** End on the Dashboard or a clean closing frame.

---

## Closing Notes

- Keep energy conversational and confident — not salesy
- Speak at a natural pace; don't rush the feature tour
- If you go over 3 minutes, cut Scene 5 to just invoicing + compliance (drop billing and settings)
- Record in one take if possible — minor stumbles are fine, authenticity matters more than polish
